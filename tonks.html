Nymphadora Tonks

Nymphadora Tonks was a metamorphmagus witch who married Remus Lupin and died during the Battle of Hogwarts. She was well known or being able to change her physical appearance (often her hair to bubble gum pink)

Also known as: Tonks, Dora

Born: 1973

Died: 2 May, 1998

Blood Status: half-blood

House: Hufflepuff

Gender: female

Wand: unknown

Patronus: Jack Rabbit, Wolf

Occupation: Auror

Loyalty: Auror Office, Ministry of Magic, Order of the Phoenix, Hogwarts.
