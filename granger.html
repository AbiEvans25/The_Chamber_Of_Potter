Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger is a muggle-born witch who was known well for being highly intelligent and becoming friends with Harry Potter. JK Rowling (author of books) has stated that Hermione reflects her younger self, with her insecurity and fear of failure.

Also known as: Minister Hermione Jean Granger

Born: 19 September, 1979

Blood Status: Muggle-born

House: Gryffindor

Gender: female

Wand: 10¾", vine wood, dragon heartstring

Patronus: otter

Occupation: Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures (formerly)

Deputy Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement (formerly)

Minister for Magic (presently)

Hogwarts: prefect, SPEW, Dumbledore’s Army
