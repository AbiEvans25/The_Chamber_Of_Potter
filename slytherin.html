Slytherin is one of the four Hogwarts houses founded by Salazar Slytherin. The traits associated with members of the Slytherin house are:

  • Resourcefulness
  • Cunning
  • Ambitious
  • Determination
  • Self-Preservation
The house is represented with the colours Green and Silver accompanied by a snake. Slytherin is usually associated with Dark Wizards and the Death Eaters.
