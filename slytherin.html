Slytherin

Slytherin is one of the four Hogwarts houses founded by Salazar Slytherin. The traits associated with members of the Slytherin house are:

  • Resourcefulness
  • Cunning
  • Ambitious
  • Determination
  • Self-Preservation
The house is represented with the colours Green and Silver accompanied by a snake. Slytherin is usually associated with Dark Wizards and the Death Eaters. The Slytherin common room is located in the dungeons of the Hogwarts Castle which is positioned underneath the Black Lake.
