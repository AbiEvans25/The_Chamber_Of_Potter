Draco Malfoy

Draco Malfoy is a pureblood wizard who developed a rivalry with Harry Potter over their time at Hogwarts particularly due to his beliefs about blood status.

Also known as: Draco Lucius Malfoy

Born: 5 June, 1980

Blood Status: Pure-blood

House: Slytherin

Gender: male

Wand: 10", Hawthorn, unicorn hair (formerly), 18", Elm, dragon heartstring

Patronus: none

Occupation: death eater (previously)

Hogwarts: prefect, seeker for Slytherin team, Inquisitorial Squad
