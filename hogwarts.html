Hogwarts

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is the British school for young witches and wizards, located in the Highlands of Scotland. The school is situated near the loch and appears to muggles as an old abandoned castle. The school was founded in the 10th century by Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw and Salazar Slytherin. The schools moto is Drac Dormeins Nuquam Titillandus which translated from Latin is Never Tickle a Sleeping Dragon. At Hogwarts, the students learn a host of subjects such as:

  • Transfiguration
  • Defence Against the Dark Arts
  • Charms
  • Astronomy
  • Potions
  • Herbology
  • Divination
And a host of others. The students at Hogwarts stay in school for 7 years doing their O.W.L’s at the end of the 5th year and N.E.W.T’s at the end of the 7th year.
