Newt Scamander

Newt Scamander was a famous Magizoologist and author of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them. He became a respected expert in the field of Magizoology after writing the book.

Also known as: Newt, Newton Artemis Fido Scamander

Born: Between 1 January and 6 December, 1897

Blood Status: half-blood or pure-blood

House: Hufflepuff

Gender: male

Wand: unknown

Patronus: unknown

Occupation: Beast Division of the Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures Magizoologist

Loyalty: British Ministry of Magic Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures Order of Merlin
