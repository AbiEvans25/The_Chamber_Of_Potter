Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw is one of the four Hogwarts houses founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. The traits associated with members of the Ravencaw house are:

  • Intelligence
  • Wisdom
  • Creativity
  • Wit
  • Acceptance
The house is represented with the colours Blue and Silver accompanied by a snake. Ravenclaw is usually associated with inteligence and the book smart wizards. The Ravenclaw common room is located in Ravenclaw Tower up the spiral stares of the Hogwarts Castle. Some norable members of the house include:
