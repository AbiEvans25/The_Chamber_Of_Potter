Gryffindor

Gryffindor is one of the four houses founded by Godric Gryffindor. The traits associated with members of the Gryffindor house:

  • Bravery
  • Courage
  • Nerve
  • Daring
  • Chivalry
The house is represented with the colours red and gold accompanied by a lion. Gryffindors are associated with heroes. The Gryffindor common room is located in Gryffindor Tower and is guarded by the Fat Lady painting who onky permits entry after giving the correct password

Some notable members of this house are:

  • Albus Dumbledore 1892 - 1899
  • Harry Potter 1991 - 1997
  • Neville Longbottom 1991 - 1998
